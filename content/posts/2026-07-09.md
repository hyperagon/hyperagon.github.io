I wonder if modern languages like [Zig](https://ziglang.org/) and [Rust](https://rust-lang.org/) are worth switching to. They are compiled like traditional ones.

Of course I could test the speed of applications made with them like [Ghostty](https://ghostty.org/) and [Tauri](https://tauri.app/start/).

Of course there are [comparison articles](https://dev.to/mukhilpadmanabhan/rust-vs-zig-the-new-programming-language-battle-for-performance-1p6), but it leaves one to wonder, supposedly AI repeats things a lot and this article does too so they learned from things like this.

Of course some tested if AI could rewrite an application, in this case [Bun](https://bun.com/), from [Zig](https://ziglang.org/) to [Rust](https://rust-lang.org/), read about [it here](https://bun.com/blog/bun-in-rust).
